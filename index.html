Recaptiko

Easy Record

Introducing a screen recording tool that simplifies your workflow like never before. Effortlessly record any screen, page, or tab with just a few clicks. When you're done, the recording automatically downloads, so you can instantly share or save it without hassle.

Features

Recaptiko is packed with a range of powerful features designed to make your screen recording experience seamless

Free & easy to use

At Recaptiko, we believe in simplicity and transparency. Record and download your screen recordings completely free of charge, without any hidden fees or surprises. Our small team of developers is dedicated to providing you with an easy-to-use and efficient screen recording service.

Customization and Control

Tailor your screen recording experience to suit your needs with Recaptiko's versatile recording options. Choose to record your entire screen for comprehensive demonstrations or tutorials, a specific window for focused application walkthroughs,Capture every keystroke, mouse click, and screen transition with precision using our intuitive control panel.

Open Source

At Recaptiko, we believe in fostering a collaborative and transparent environment. That's why we plan to make our screen recording platform open source, allowing you to contribute your expertise and ideas to help us create an even more powerful tool. By becoming an open-source platform, we enable developers to explore Recaptiko's features.

If you're reading this, you're undoubtedly wondering who the target audience is for this product. That's great news, because it applies to everyone. Feel free to reach out to us to sponsor this project or to contribute to it in any way.
